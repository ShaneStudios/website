Guidelines

Review Guidelines

Thank you for taking the time to provide a review! Your feedback is incredibly valuable for improving this website and my projects. To ensure your review is as helpful as possible, please consider the following guidelines.

The Principles of a Constructive Review

A great review goes beyond a simple "I liked it" or "I didn't like it." It provides context and specific examples that help me understand your experience. A constructive review is:

  • Specific: Instead of "the articles page is confusing," try "I found it difficult to find the search bar on the articles page." Specificity helps me pinpoint the exact problem.
  • Objective: Try to describe your experience factually. For example, "The RyxIDE demo page took 10 seconds to load on my connection," is more useful than "the page is slow."
  • Balanced: If possible, mention both what you liked and what you think could be improved. This provides a well-rounded perspective.
  • Actionable: The best feedback includes a potential solution or a clear problem statement. "It would be helpful if the 'Copy' button on snippets turned green after clicking" is a great, actionable suggestion.
  • Respectful: All feedback is welcome, but please maintain a respectful and considerate tone.

What to Review?

You can review any aspect of Shane Studios, including:

  • The overall website design and user experience.
  • The quality and clarity of a specific article.
  • The functionality and usefulness of a project or snippet.
  • The performance and responsiveness of the site.

Please be sure to mention what specific page, article, or project you are reviewing in your email!

Star Rating Guide

When you submit a review, please include a star rating from 1 to 5. Here is a general guide to help you decide on a rating:

Rating Description
★★★★★ Excellent. The feature/content is exceptional, polished, and exceeded expectations. I would highly recommend it to others. No major issues found.
★★★★☆ Good. A solid and useful feature/content with a few minor issues or areas for improvement. I had a positive experience overall.
★★★☆☆ Average. The feature/content works as expected but isn't exceptional. It may have some noticeable usability issues or lacks polish, but it's functional.
★★☆☆☆ Below Average. The feature/content has significant issues that hinder the experience. It may be confusing, buggy, or incomplete.
★☆☆☆☆ Poor. The feature/content is broken, unusable, or fundamentally flawed. It caused significant frustration.
